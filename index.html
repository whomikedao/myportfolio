About me

I’m a person with high curiosity that loves to have an understanding of the universe and what it has to offer. I went to University of Houston for Physics and was introduced to the world of Computer Science and immediately fell in love with it’s complexion and similarities to physics, and applications to this quick evolving modern world. Creating and manipulating solutions has always been the foundation of my fun and ultimately became an undeniable passion. I put a huge emphasis on understanding the foundation for any sort of knowledge to better understand why things work. With the combination of my competitive side that drives my self-development forward—the two support each other hand in hand.

I’m a bit monotoned when I speak, some say I might be a robot or just an advanced AI that could create its own siblings on the web, who knows? But whatever it may be, there’s a chance that I learn as though I am immortal. So, at this moment I believe it is more impressive to create something from scratch than it is to improve beyond something and that to me is the beauty of the field of software developers. But...

"With great power comes great responsiblity."
